Why this project?

While working for the DX Group, they received an award from UConn's COVID-19 Rapid Seed Funding (COVID-RSF) Program for the creation of a website and dynamic dashboard to showcase the University's response to COVID-19. I was able to take the lead for design and develop the site through the Universityâ€™s Aurora/Wordpress service. The main goal was to convey the information clearly to the audience who were mainly students. I did this by designing many of the diagrams and figures myself so that they were easy to follow along for students who do not know too much about the content.

As a result of this site having quite a bit of traffic, it was crucial for me to make sure that many web accessibility techniques were applied in order to reach all of the users who come across this site. Another important aspect I kept in mind while designing and developing was making sure it was as easy to browse through on mobile as it is on desktop because university students are much more likely to open sites on their phones.

Style Guide
